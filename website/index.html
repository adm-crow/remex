How it works

From folder to knowledge base in minutes

Three steps. No configuration. No cloud account.

1

Ingest your documents

Point Remex at any folder. It reads PDFs, Word files, Markdown, spreadsheets, code and more — chunking and embedding everything locally on your machine.

2

Search semantically

Ask a question in plain English. Vector similarity finds the most relevant passages across one or more collections — no keyword matching required.

3

Get AI-synthesised answers

Connect Anthropic, OpenAI, or a local Ollama instance to get a synthesised answer grounded in your files — with every source cited.
